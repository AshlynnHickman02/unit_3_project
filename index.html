    What is the Poke-Web?

    This website was created so people do not have to try and catch all the Pokemon and they can view them in their own time.
    It will include basic info such as evolution, pokedex number, name, level, type, strengths, weaknesses, and basic moves they can use.

    How Do I Use Your Site?

    Each page will have links on the starter Pokemon and their evolved form to navigate through the site you can click on the text or image link to get to the desired pokemon.
